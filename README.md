# Warehouse Automation Platform

Business automation and warehouse management platform integrated with Bitrix24 CRM.

The platform was developed to automate warehouse operations, inventory synchronization, pricing logic, sales workflows, and operational reporting for real-world business environments.

---

# Overview

This system combines warehouse management, CRM synchronization, workflow automation, and operational reporting into a single platform connected with Bitrix24 through APIs and webhooks.

The project focuses on solving operational and business challenges related to:

* roll and meter product sales
* dynamic pricing
* inventory synchronization
* warehouse allocation
* automated workflows
* reporting and operational visibility

## Smart Warehouse Allocation

The platform includes automatic warehouse allocation logic.

For example:

* if a leftover piece of material already matches the requested order size,
* the system prioritizes using that leftover instead of opening a new roll.

This helps optimize warehouse operations and reduce material waste.
